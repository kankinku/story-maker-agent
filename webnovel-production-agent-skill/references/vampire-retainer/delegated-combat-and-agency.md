# Delegated Combat And Agency

Use this reference when retainers, summons, or subordinates perform most direct combat.

## Core Problem

If retainers fight everything, the protagonist can become passive. Preserve agency by giving the protagonist strategy, staffing, resource allocation, target selection, and reputation response.

## Delegated Combat Loop

```text
protagonist investigates target and reward
-> cross-checks information
-> assigns retainers by role
-> buys or grants equipment
-> deploys scouting or shared vision
-> unexpected threat appears
-> retainer solves through distinct capability
-> loot and witness appear
-> protagonist chooses reinvestment or next assignment
```

## Protagonist Decisions

Leave these decisions to the protagonist:

- hunting area investigation
- information cross-check
- retainer assignment
- equipment purchase
- blood power and essence investment
- evolution priority
- rescue target selection
- scouting network operation
- community response
- tower reward choice
- territory and organization management

## Retainer Design

```yaml
retainer:
  combat_role: ""
  personality: ""
  devotion_style: ""
  domestic_role: ""
  growth_need: ""
  misunderstanding_effect: ""
```

Devotion must differ by retainer. A cold executor, crafter, cheerful youngest, scout, and tactician should not all express loyalty the same way.

## Anti-Patterns

- Retainer solves route, weakness, equipment, and execution without protagonist decision.
- New retainer is a complete upgrade over old retainers.
- Retainers have identical speech and worship style.
- Protagonist's contribution is reduced to "send them."
