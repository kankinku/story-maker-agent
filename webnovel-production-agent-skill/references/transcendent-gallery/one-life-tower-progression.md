# One Life Tower Progression

Use this reference for tower-climb growth where failure is death, not a reset loop.

## Difference From Retry Loops

This is not a repeated-failure structure. Growth comes from:

```text
prior information
+ repetitive training
+ field observation
+ others' experience
+ instant decision
= one irreversible clear
```

## Preparation Requirements

Before a major survival victory, stack some of:

- technique practice
- indirect information about enemy family or stage type
- tools from prior stages
- mentor warning
- protagonist's prior experience
- rule discovered at the scene
- NPC's local knowledge

Victory should feel like accumulated preparation combining at one moment, not a hidden overpowered skill.

## Growth Stages

```text
rare newbie
-> protected junior
-> trainee who understands advice
-> field fighter who modifies advice
-> talent who surprises mentors
-> leader of other climbers
-> figure who updates transcendent records
```

Early story asks: "What should I do?"

Middle story asks: "Which advice applies here?"

Late story asks: "Why did this result happen, and what did it prove?"

## Stage Variation

Avoid a fixed pattern of:

```text
ask for information -> boss fight -> video upload
```

Vary stages through:

- training bottleneck
- NPC emotional dilemma
- environmental puzzle
- cooperation with another climber
- reputation mismatch
- stage rule with no clean answer
- victory that creates loss
